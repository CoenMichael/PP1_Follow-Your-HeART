About Us

Set in an idyllic studio on Galways wild coastline, 'follow your heART' is an art school dedicated to improving your creativity. Whether it is your first time with a pencil or a seasoned artist ready to tackle the techniques of the Old Masters, we have something for all skill levels. We believe that learning in a relaxed and fun environment truly benefits not only your artistic skills but also our mental and creative state of mind. All materials are provided and we also have free hot and cold beverages at each class. So why wait? Follow your heart and learn some art!
